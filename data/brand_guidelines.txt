Brand Voice & Tone
Our brand communicates with confidence, clarity, and empathy. All campaign copy should feel approachable yet authoritative. Avoid jargon unless speaking to a technical audience, and always prioritize the customer's success story. Headlines should be punchy and benefit-driven, while body copy provides enough detail to build trust without overwhelming the reader.

Target ROAS & Financial Goals
The minimum acceptable Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) for any paid campaign is 4.0x. Campaigns falling below this threshold for more than seven consecutive days must be flagged for immediate review. For prospecting campaigns, we aim for a 3.0x ROAS within the first fourteen days, while retargeting campaigns should achieve at least 6.0x. Any campaign projected to miss these targets by more than 20% requires budget reallocation or creative refresh.

Channel Preferences & Allocation
We prioritize Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads for upper-funnel awareness due to strong visual storytelling capabilities. Google Search remains our primary capture channel for high-intent queries. TikTok is approved for experimental Gen-Z outreach with a capped test budget of $5,000 per month. LinkedIn is reserved for B2B enterprise campaigns only. No spend should be directed toward untested channels without explicit written approval from the Growth Lead.

Performance Thresholds & Guardrails
Click-Through Rate (CTR) must remain above 1.2% for display campaigns and above 3.5% for search campaigns. Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) should not exceed $45 for consumer products and $120 for SaaS trials. Frequency caps are set at 3 impressions per user per day on social channels to prevent ad fatigue. If CTR drops below 0.8% for three consecutive days, pause the ad set and diagnose creative fatigue or audience mismatch immediately.

Creative & Compliance Standards
All creative assets must include a clear call-to-action (CTA) and adhere to platform-specific safe-zone guidelines. Disclaimers are mandatory for any promotional offer exceeding 30% off. We maintain a strict no-clickbait policy; headlines must accurately reflect the landing-page experience. Dark patterns, misleading urgency timers, and false scarcity claims are prohibited and will result in immediate campaign termination.

Budget Management Rules
Daily budgets should not fluctuate by more than 25% without a 48-hour learning-phase warning. Campaigns with fewer than 50 conversions per week are considered statistically under-powered and should be consolidated into broader ad sets where possible. Monthly spend must stay within 10% of the approved media plan; any variance beyond that requires a written variance report to Finance by the 5th of the following month.

Reporting & Accountability
Weekly performance reports are due every Monday by 10:00 AM. Reports must include spend, ROAS, CTR, impressions, conversions, and a narrative summary of optimizations made. Red flags—defined as any metric breaching the thresholds above—must be highlighted in red and accompanied by a remediation plan. Opportunities for scale should be highlighted in green and include projected incremental revenue and required budget.
